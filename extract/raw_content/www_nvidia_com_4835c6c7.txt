The Must-See Highlights
-----------------------




![]()




### NVIDIA Booth

Get hands-on with the latest NVIDIA technology and developer tools in a high-energy demo space designed for real-world workflows, from data center to edge and beyond.






![]()




### Expo Theater

Drop in to the Expo Theater for rapid-fire sessions, live product showcases, and partner spotlights that bring breakthrough use cases and success stories to life right on the show floor.













 


Explore the Innovation Pavilions
--------------------------------




Navigate a curated set of pavilions that connect you with experts, solutions, and peers in your industry. Each pavilion features interactive demos and real customer stories so you can see innovation in action.





![]()




### Automotive

Visit this pavilion to see the latest cutting-edge AI technologies transforming the future of transportation and mobility.






![]()




### DSX AI Infrastructure

Discover how leaders across power and cooling, construction, and software are building large-scale AI factories using unified NVIDIA DSX standards and the Vera Rubin platform.






![]()




### Financial Services

Explore cutting-edge AI use cases, from algorithmic trading to agentic commerce in payments, showcasing how AI is transforming the industry.









![]()




### Healthcare and Life Sciences

Learn about AI-driven breakthroughs in biology, surgical guidance, and intelligent patient care.






![]()




### Inception Startups

See how [NVIDIA Inception](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/startups/?ncid=ref-inc-711943-vt33) is helping over 30,000 global technology startups accelerate their applications. We’re proud to showcase 55+ of them in the Inception Startup Pavilion.






![]()




### Industrial AI and Robotics

Learn how leaders across factories and robotics are scaling autonomous operations with industrial AI, digital twins, and accelerated computing.









![]()




### Quantum Computing

Discover how companies are developing and supporting all aspects of tomorrow’s quantum-GPU supercomputer, including quantum hardware, software, and application developers.






![]()




### Telecommunications

See how industry leaders are transforming telecom operations, advancing wireless networks, and deploying sovereign AI infrastructure.
